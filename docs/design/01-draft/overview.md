# Design Overview

**Project Name:** GoalPost  
**Started At:** 12 Feb 2026

## Introduction

GoalPost is a comprehensive solution designed to assist individuals in achieving their personal goals through structured planning and AI-driven insights. It tackles common challenges in personal development by offering a clear path to goal attainment.

- **Setup a Goal for Yourself**: Define your aspirations with clarity.
- **AI-Assisted Achievement**: Utilize artificial intelligence to identify the most efficient path to successfully reach your goals.
- **Create Plans and Milestones**: Break down goals into achievable milestones, providing a roadmap to success.
- **See Your Progression**: Monitor your progress over time, ensuring you stay on track.
- **Capture and Share Your Success**: Document your achievements and share them with friends to amplify motivation.

## Goals

### Establish System

Designed to enhance various aspects of personal life by using a systematic approach. Key problems addressed include:

- **Routine Activity**: Difficulty in maintaining consistent habits. Solved through daily routine suggestions and tracking.
- **Progression Tracking**: Lack of awareness of progress. Solved through detailed progress reports and analytics.
- **Gamification / Reward System**: Low motivation. Addressed by incorporating rewards and achievements to encourage continued engagement.
- **Time Scheduling**: Poor time management skills. Resolved using intuitive scheduling tools and reminders.
- **Reminder / Notification**: Forgetfulness. Solved by automated reminders for key activities.
- **Morning / Night Review of Upcoming Activities**: Difficulty in planning. Helped by daily reviews to prepare for and reflect on daily tasks.

### Understand the Problem

Focuses on identifying and overcoming obstacles in goal attainment. Specific problems and solutions include:

- **Find Out Why the System is Not Working**: Tackles inability to identify systemic issues. Solved through AI diagnostics and feedback.
- **Journal When You Failed to Complete Goals**: Offers insight into recurring issues by encouraging reflective journaling.
- **AI Assistance to Understand Failures**: Provides expert analysis on why certain goals are unmet and suggests corrective actions.

### Templates and Modules

Provides customizable templates for various life aspects, addressing the problem of starting from scratch in complex areas:

- **Fitness**: Achieve fitness objectives using tailored workout and nutrition plans.
- **Financial**: Manage personal finances with expert budgeting and saving guides.
- **Education**: Enhance learning with structured educational modules.
- **Diet**: Personalize diet plans for optimal health.
- **Beauty**: Attain beauty goals through specialized care routines.
- **Health**: Maintain overall health with proactive health management tips.

### Sharing Your Success

Encourages networking and emotional support by enabling you to:

- **Capture Photos of Your Success**: Document achievements visually to strengthen commitment.
- **Share Them with Your Friends**: Foster a support network to celebrate milestones together.
- **Showcase Your Progression**: Publicly track growth to inspire yourself and others.
