# GoalPost — a quick, friendly intro

Welcome to GoalPost, your no-nonsense sidekick for turning “someday” into “done.” It helps you pick a direction, make a plan, show up daily, learn from misses, and celebrate the wins. All with a little AI wisdom to keep you moving.

## Why you’ll like it
- Clarity without complexity: set goals, break them down, and know exactly what to do next.
- Momentum on autopilot: daily reviews, nudges, and routines keep you consistent.
- Smarter every week: reflect, fix what’s not working, and level up with AI diagnostics.
- Motivation built in: progress charts, streaks, and small wins that actually feel good.

## The vibe (fast overview of what’s inside)
- Goals, plans, and milestones: map your big goals into step-by-step milestones you can actually do.
- Routines & habits: track the small daily actions that make the big things happen.
- Scheduling & reminders: add your tasks, get nudged at the right time, and keep your day sane.
- Morning & night reviews: 2–5 minute check-ins to plan the day and reflect at night.
- Progress & analytics: watch your momentum build with streaks, completion rates, and trend lines.
- Gamification & rewards: earn points, unlock badges, and keep the dopamine honest.
- Journaling & failure reflection: capture what tripped you up and convert it into a plan.
- AI assistance: get guidance, planning help, and “why did this slip?” diagnostics.
- Templates & domain modules: jump-start with plug-and-play setups for fitness, finance, learning, diet, beauty, and health.
- Success capture & sharing: snap wins, document milestones, and share highlights (only if you want to).
- Settings, privacy, data control: your data, your rules—export, delete, and tune notifications anytime.

## Who it’s for
- The busy pro who wants a clearer plan and fewer dropped balls.
- The student who needs structure without stress.
- The athlete-in-progress who thrives on streaks and feedback loops.
- The creator who ships more when the path is visible.
- The reflective achiever who learns from misses and compounds wins.

## A day with GoalPost
1) Morning: quick review → today’s top priorities and habits, locked in.  
2) Do the work: milestones + routines, tracked as you go.  
3) Nudges: timely reminders keep you honest (and human).  
4) Night: 2-minute reflection → note wins, log frictions, and adjust tomorrow.  
5) Weekly: see trends, tweak plans, feel the momentum.

## AI, but actually helpful
- Planning: turn fuzzy goals into concrete milestones and weekly actions.
- Diagnostics: when things slip, get root-cause ideas and suggested fixes.
- Coaching: gentle prompts to reduce overload, right-size tasks, and keep focus.

## Templates you can grab on day one
- Fitness: routines, workouts, and recovery checkpoints.
- Financial: budgeting tasks, saving targets, and review cadences.
- Education: study plans, spaced reviews, and project milestones.
- Diet: meal patterns, hydration, and habit loops.
- Beauty & health: routines and check-ins that don’t feel like a chore.

(Templates are starting points—tweak freely.)

## Privacy and control (the grown-up bits)
- You own your data: export and delete anytime.
- Sharing is opt-in: nothing goes public unless you say so.
- Notifications your way: fully configurable by time, channel, and category.

## MVP promise (what’s in v1)
- Create goals, plans, and milestones
- Track routines and habits
- Schedule tasks with reminders
- Morning + night reviews
- Basic progress analytics and streaks
- Gamification: points/badges (foundations)
- Journaling and failure reflection
- AI guidance and diagnostics (core flows)
- Starter templates for key domains
- Success capture and optional sharing
- Settings for privacy, backup/export, and notifications

## What’s not the focus right now
- Team/collab features, advanced integrations, and complex workflows are intentionally out of scope for the early version—we’re laser-focused on personal outcomes first.

## Quick start (3 steps)
1) Create one goal you actually care about.  
2) Add 2–3 milestones and your first week of actions.  
3) Turn on morning + night reviews. That’s your momentum engine.

## TL;DR
Plan clearly, act daily, learn fast, and celebrate wins. GoalPost makes consistency easier—and consistency makes everything else possible.
